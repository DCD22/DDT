Who am I?

Good question!
Deciding to gain your driving licence and make the right decisions, you want to know that you're getting information from the people in the know.


My name is Dan, I'm 30 and I currently hold the position of Training and Recruitment Officer for a public transport provider in the United Kingdom. My main role is to take people with no PCV driving experience and make them a safe and confident driver with the knowledge and skills to achieve their vocational PCV licence enabling them to become bus drivers.


I've recieved extensive training in adult learning and training techniques for both classroom environments and out on the road, I work in strict compliance with the DVSA to ensure the best outcome for my trainees.

What is Dan's Driver Training?

I decided to put Dan's Driver Training together to be a one-stop-shop resource for learning to drive and obtaining additional vocational licences, keeping up to date with the latest information and rule changes from the DVSA and DVLA.


Any and all information on this website has been checked and double checked to ensure there are no mistakes in the informaion.
